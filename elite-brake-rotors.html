Are Elite brake pads suitable for daily use? ⌄
Yes, Elite brake pads are designed to balance performance and comfort, making them suitable for everyday driving as well as more demanding conditions.

Yes, Elite brake pads are engineered to perform reliably under higher temperatures, ensuring consistent braking even during extended or heavy use.

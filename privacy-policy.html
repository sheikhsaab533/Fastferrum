Privacy Policy

JUMP JUICE

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

Jump Juice ("we," "us," or "our") is a Canadian consumer electronics brand based in Mississauga, Ontario, that designs and sells vehicle jump starters. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website at [www.jumpjuice.com] (the "Website").

Our Website is an informational and product display platform only. You cannot purchase products directly from us online — there is no checkout, no payment system, and no account registration. When you're ready to buy, we redirect you to trusted third-party retailers such as Amazon.

We are committed to being transparent about our data practices and to respecting your privacy in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate our Website and respond to your questions.

A. Information You Provide to Us

If you contact us through our Contact / Support form, you may submit:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • A subject line
  • Your message or inquiry

This information is used solely to respond to your support request. We do not use it for marketing, newsletters, promotional campaigns, or advertising purposes.

B. Information Collected Automatically

Google Analytics automatically collects certain non-personal, technical information when you visit our Website.

  • Device type and browser information
  • Pages viewed and time spent on the Website
  • Referring website or search engine
  • General geographic location (country or city level)
  • Aggregated demographic insights
  • IP-based analytics data used in aggregate form

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only for limited purposes, including:

  • Responding to customer support inquiries
  • Analyzing website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improving Website functionality and performance
  • Identifying technical issues and maintaining security

We do not:

  • Send marketing emails or newsletters
  • Run advertising or remarketing campaigns
  • Create user profiles
  • Sell, rent, or trade personal information

3. Cookies and Google Analytics

We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or personalization purposes. However, Google Analytics uses standard analytics cookies to collect anonymized aggregate data regarding Website usage.

These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and are not used for marketing.

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking through Google's Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On or manage cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Links and Marketplaces

Our Website contains links to third-party retailers such as Amazon and other authorized sellers through our "Where to Buy" feature.

Once you leave our Website, the privacy practices of those external websites are governed by their own policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting personal information or making purchases.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information.

Information may only be shared:

  • With Google as our analytics provider
  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Jump Juice or its users

Contact form submissions are never sold or shared for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries and manage reasonable follow-up communication.

Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention settings.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through our Website. Data transmitted through our contact form is protected using HTTPS encryption.

While we strive to safeguard information, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable Canadian privacy laws, you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request access to your information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Request deletion of personal information subject to legal obligations

Residents of Quebec may also have additional rights under Law 25.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has submitted information through our Website, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements, business practices, or Website functionality.

Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date shown at the top of this page.

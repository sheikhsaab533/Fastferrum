FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Are Pro brake pads suitable for regular city driving? ⌄
Yes, Pro brake pads can be used for city driving, but they are primarily designed for higher performance conditions. While they work well in daily use, their full benefits are best experienced during more demanding driving.
Do they handle extreme heat? ⌄
Yes, Pro brake pads are built to withstand high temperatures. They maintain consistent performance even under heavy braking and prolonged use, making them ideal for demanding conditions.
Is braking response more aggressive? ⌄
Yes, Pro brake pads offer a more responsive and aggressive braking feel. They provide quicker bite and stronger stopping power compared to standard brake pads.
